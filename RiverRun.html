River Run

When you enter this split level home you will head down a few stairs to enter an open layout which encompasses the great room, dining, and kitchen spaces. The bedrooms and bathrooms are located on the top floor. The first room offers two double beds, a flat screen TV and large picture windows surrounded by nature. Right down the hall is a half bathroom followed by a full bathroom and the master suite. The master suite is equipped with a king size bed, flat screen TV, and large picture windows facing the Blanco River. There is a bonus seating area with futon off the master bedroom. All bedrooms have both pulldown shades and curtains and closets. This property has several outdoor seating areas both covered and uncovered. Directly outside of the kitchen is a covered patio seating area with ceiling fan and surrounded by cafe lights. The whole house sound system extends to the covered patio. From the patio you can head down the property to an open air seating platform over looking the river. Both the covered patio and open air space are excellent places to observe the local deer feeding (deer feed provided). If you continue towards the river from the open air seating area you'll come across the properties large fire pit, the perfect gathering place to relax and watch the river go by. A short walk from the fireplace are two sets of stairs with two separate easy access points into the river.
